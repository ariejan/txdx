Description
TxDx is a desktop Todo app that fully supports the Todo.txt format.

* You own your data. In plain text.
* Syncs with Dropbox, Syncthing, Nextcloud or whatever you're already using.
* Offers the power of entering your todo's in plain text, but it looks nice.
